## System

AI Daily Pulse runs as a fixed daily composition:

1. **Edition frame** — date, reading time, and a clear path through the page.
2. **Three things to know** — the highest-signal points before anything else.
3. **Featured story** — one narrative given space, with a source link out.
4. **Signal feed** — filtered cards across pulse, product, research and infra.

## Result

AI Daily Pulse turns a scattered monitoring habit into a productized daily edition. The design priority is orientation, not completeness: finish the pulse, then go deep only where it matters.
