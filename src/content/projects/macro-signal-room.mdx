## System

Macro Signal Room is structured as a single operating view:

1. **The call** — the primary thesis, confidence, and last update.
2. **Since last update** — what changed, what stays, and why it matters.
3. **Next tests** — timed questions that would confirm or challenge the view.
4. **Bigger picture** — inflation, policy, rates, and risk as the drivers behind the room.

## Result

Macro Signal Room turns a private research habit into a productized desk. The design priority is orientation: know the call, know what moved, know what to watch next.
